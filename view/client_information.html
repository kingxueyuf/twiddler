Normal Information

The University of Wisconsin–La Crosse (also known as UW–La Crosse, La Crosse, or UW–L) is a public university located in La Crosse, Wisconsin, USA. It is a comprehensive four-year institution within the University of Wisconsin System awarding bachelor's, master's, and one doctoral degree. UW–La Crosse offers 91 undergraduate programs in 44 disciplines,[5] and 26 graduate programs with emphases in eight disciplines.
